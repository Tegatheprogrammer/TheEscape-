Description

THE ESCAPE follows Eseosa Iyamu, who gets imprisoned to break his brother, Bradley, out of death row. Using a tattoo with escape plans, he navigates danger and uncovers a conspiracy. The series is filled with suspense, action, and family loyalty.

Cast

  • Eseosa Iyamu as Ben Barber
  • Bradley as James Payton
  • Davido Raindow as Angela Payton
  • Laurence Fishburne as Omar
  • John Leguizamo as Santiago

Director

*THE ESCAPE* was directed by ALEXANDER MATHEW and others . Known for *Barbershop* and the *Fantastic Four* series, thier directing style blends humor and action seamlessly.

Producer

Produced by Kevin Hooks, a Hollywood powerhouse behind hits like *Think Like a Man* and *Girls Trip*.
