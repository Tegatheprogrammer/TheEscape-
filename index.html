Description

Jayden, a restless teenager, wanders through an abandoned school at night, haunted by whispers taunting him about being trapped. He stumbles upon a mysterious room lined with cracked mirrors, each reflecting distorted versions of himself. He finds himself in a dark room filled with mirrors, each showing a different version of himself. As a mysterious voice echoes around him, he’s forced to confront his fears, doubts, and the expectations that define him. Is he trapped in a dream, or is this the key to finding his true self?
